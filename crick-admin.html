CrickAdmin – Smart Box Cricket Booking & Turf Management App

CrickAdmin is a powerful Flutter-based mobile app designed for cricket facility owners and managers. The app provides complete control over box cricket bookings, slot management, turf scheduling, and revenue tracking, ensuring smooth operations and enhanced customer satisfaction.

  • Real-Time Booking Management: Monitor live and upcoming cricket bookings instantly through a centralized admin dashboard.
  • Calendar-Based Slot Scheduling: Visualize daily, weekly, and monthly bookings with color-coded time slots for easy management and conflict reduction.
  • Detailed Booking & Customer Insights: Access player names, contact numbers, booking duration, and payment details in one place.
  • Instant Notifications & Alerts: Receive real-time updates for new bookings, cancellations, reschedules, and slot changes.
  • Earnings & Revenue Tracking: Track daily, weekly, and monthly income with clear reports for better financial visibility.
  • Manual & Walk-In Booking Support: Quickly add offline or walk-in bookings directly from the admin app without external tools.
  • Flexible Pricing & Turf Settings: Configure pitch availability, time-based pricing, peak-hour rates, and box capacity with ease.
  • Multi-Box & Multi-Turf Management: Manage multiple cricket boxes or turf locations from a single app interface.
  • Secure & Scalable Architecture: Designed for reliability, fast performance, and secure data handling to support growing cricket facilities.
