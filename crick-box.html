CrickBox – Box Cricket Booking Mobile App

CrickBox is a user-friendly mobile app that simplifies box cricket discovery and slot booking. Players can find nearby cricket boxes, check slot availability, book instantly, and get real-time notifications without any online payment hassles.

  • Nearby Box Cricket Discovery: Find cricket boxes near your location with complete details and pricing.
  • Detailed Box Listings: View photos, pitch information, address, and slot-wise pricing (day/night).
  • Date & Time Slot Booking: Check real-time availability and book preferred slots instantly.
  • Payment-Free Reservation: Reserve slots without online payments and pay directly to the box owner.
  • Map & Location Navigation: One-tap map redirection for easy ground navigation.
  • Fast & Intuitive User Experience: Clean UI with smooth navigation across all devices.
  • Booking Status Notifications: Instant alerts when a booking is approved, rejected, canceled, or for reminders.

Features & Benefits

CrickBox offers a seamless and reliable box cricket booking experience, helping players quickly find nearby grounds, reserve slots, and stay updated with real-time notifications.

Location Based Box Cricket Discovery

Easily find nearby box cricket grounds with detailed listings, photos, pricing, and pitch information for quick decision-making.

Discover Nearby Cricket Boxes

View cricket boxes on an interactive map with pins displayed within a 10 km radius from the user’s current location, making it easy to find and choose nearby grounds instantly.

Real-Time Slot Booking

Check live slot availability and reserve preferred day or night time slots instantly with a smooth, user-friendly booking flow.

Detailed Box Listings

Get full box details with images, pitch information, location map, owner contact, and slot-wise pricing for day, night, and weekend bookings—helping users make quick and confident booking decisions.

Instant Booking Status Notifications

Receive real-time notifications for booking approval, rejection, cancellation, and timely match reminders.
