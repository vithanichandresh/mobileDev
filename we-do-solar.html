Features & Benefits

WeDoSolar’s smart mobile platform connects users to their solar panels in real time — combining IoT, AWS, and Flutter for a seamless energy monitoring experience.

Real-Time Solar Data Monitoring

Users can view live energy production, consumption, and CO₂ savings directly from their connected solar panels. The system ensures accurate, instant data updates via IoT integration.

Smart Device Connectivity

The app securely connects with IoT-enabled solar panels through AWS IoT Core, allowing seamless device synchronization and reliable data transmission.

Interactive Energy Insights

Beautiful, data-driven charts visualize daily, weekly, and monthly energy performance. Users gain actionable insights into power generation and efficiency trends.

Cloud-Powered Scalability

Backed by AWS services like Lambda and API Gateway, the app efficiently handles real-time data streams, ensuring performance and scalability for thousands of users.

Seamless Cross-Platform Experience

Developed with Flutter, the app delivers a unified and responsive interface across both Android and iOS, maintaining smooth performance and consistency.

Secure & Reliable Infrastructure

With AWS-backed encryption, secure APIs, and reliable cloud storage, user data and device communication remain fully protected.
