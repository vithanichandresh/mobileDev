Features & Benefits

DSDice delivers a fast, lightweight, and beautifully animated dice-rolling experience built with Flutter—designed to provide realistic randomness, smooth performance, and engaging gameplay for users across all devices.

Tap to Roll Interaction

The app features a simple tap-to-roll mechanism that anyone can use without instructions.Users can tap anywhere on the screen or use the roll button to start a dice roll.This intuitive interaction ensures a fast and frustration-free user experience.

Multi-Orientation

DSDice is fully optimized for both portrait and landscape orientations across all devices.The layout adjusts automatically to provide a smooth and consistent rolling experience.Users can enjoy uninterrupted gameplay regardless of how they hold their device.

Realistic Dice Animation

DSDice delivers life-like dice rolling animations with a fast-to-slow motion sequence.The smooth stop effect closely resembles a real physical dice roll.This animation enhances realism while maintaining lightweight app performance.

Theme Support

DSDice allows users to switch between multiple vibrant color themes.Choose from Blue, Red, Green, and Yellow to match your personal style.Theme changes are instant, giving users a fresh and personalized app experience.

Dice Roll Sound Effects

Optional dice roll sound effects add an extra layer of engagement to each roll.Users can enable or disable the sound based on their preference.The sound is perfectly synchronized with the dice animation for realism.
